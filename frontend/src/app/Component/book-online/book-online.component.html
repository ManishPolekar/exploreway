This is Online Cab-Booking System where you can book and pay online to secure and reserve your ride with us. Online payment can be made by all popular credit cards and debit cards(Mastercard, Visa, etc).Payments are processed by Stripe Payment Gateway(a secured payment processor), no card details will be stored in our website.
To be noted,Incase of cancellation, 90% refund will be made to the same card used for payment,and the 10% will be charged as cancellation or processing fee.
Please make your booking at least 24 hours in advance.
Look at the Summary for the the actual cost, before proceeding with paymment.

- Minimum 3 hours is required for hourly booking. By requesting 1 or 2 hours ride, fee will be charged to make up the full price of 3 hours.
- For Airport Pickup/Transfers, please provide the flight number or arrival time.
- For Hourly booking, please provide the pickup time and the drop off time.
- Rs500 to Rs2,500 will be charged for advance booking, where Rs500 will be charged for extending or extra hour depending on the vehicle type.
- Rs 500 will be charged for extra per/hour depending on the vehicle type.
- 5% discount will be given for booking 3 days in advance.

You can also check out our Pricing Page before booking.
